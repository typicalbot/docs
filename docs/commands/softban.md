# Softban

Softban a member from the server.

## Permission

Requires 'Ban Members'

## Usage

 - `$softban <user>`
 - `$softban <user> [days]`
 - `$softban <user> [reason]`
 - `$softban <user> [days] [reason]`

## Examples

 - `$softban @Xenotater`
 - `$softban @Xenotater 2`
 - `$softban @Xenotater Shamelessly promoting his YouTube channel`
 - `$softban @Xenotater 2 Shamelessly promoting his YouTube channel`
